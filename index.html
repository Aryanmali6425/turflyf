ABOUT TURFLYF.

TurfLyf is an active platform looking to encourage maximum participation from people towards sports and fitness initiatives.

Sports is inside each one of you; it just needs a bit of motivation, a word of encouragement! Our mission is to provide state of the art multi-sports astro turf grounds which facilitate sports such as cricket, football, volleyball, handball; activities such as yoga, crossfit training, football coaching; events such as birthday parties, corporate team building, tournaments; along with providing high performance sporting apparels and merchandise, exhilarating active experiences, and coaching for grassroots development.

Call us now for bookings and inquiries. Play On!!
